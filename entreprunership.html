The Nature of Entrepreneurship

What is Entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is the process of creating, developing, and managing a new business venture to achieve financial success while accepting the associated risks.

Key Characteristics of Entrepreneurs

  • Innovation: Developing new ideas or improving existing ones
  • Risk-taking: Willingness to face uncertainty
  • Vision: Clear picture of what they want to achieve
  • Persistence: Determination to overcome obstacles
  • Adaptability: Ability to adjust to changing circumstances

Types of Entrepreneurs

Type Description Example
Small Business Local operations with limited growth Grocery store, restaurant
Scalable Startup Designed for rapid growth Tech companies
Social Focus on solving social problems Non-profits, social enterprises
